Privacy Policy

Welcome to ICVMS. Your privacy and the protection of your personal information are important to us. We collect information when you interact with our Services. Types of information may include:

Personal Information

Information that identifies you personally, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contact form submissions
  • Any other information you choose to provide directly

Usage and Technical Information

Automatically collected data when you visit our site, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages viewed

  • Device type and operating system

We use collected information for purposes including but not limited to:

We process your information based on legal grounds such as consent, contractual necessity, and legitimate interests.
