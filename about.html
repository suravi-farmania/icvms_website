About the ICVMS

The International Council for Vedic & Metaphysical Studies (ICVMS) is a globally recognized authority dedicated to advancing the integrity and accessibility of ancient wisdom traditions in the modern world.

Our Mission

To establish and uphold a global framework for accreditation, ensuring the highest standards in education, research, and practice within Vedic and Metaphysical sciences.

Our Vision

To be the foremost international body recognizing and promoting professionals and institutions dedicated to the authentic transmission and application of ancient spiritual wisdom.

Our Objectives

To standardize education, recognize professionals, uphold ethical practices, and ensure global acceptance of Vedic and Metaphysical studies, fostering a community of accredited experts.

A Message from Our Founders

In an era where ancient wisdom seeks modern credibility, the ICVMS was founded on the principle of bridging the profound insights of Vedic and Metaphysical sciences with rigorous academic and ethical standards. We envisioned a world where these invaluable traditions are recognized, respected, and accessible globally, guided by integrity and enlightened leadership."

Our Mission

ICVMS is dedicated to advancing Vedic and metaphysical sciences responsibly by setting high educational and ethical standards, accrediting credible practitioners and institutions, and fostering a trusted global community that empowers individuals to achieve personal growth and contribute to societal harmony.

Our Vision

Preserving Wisdom, Empowering Futures The International Council for Vedic and Metaphysical Sciences (ICVMS) envisions a world where Vedic wisdom and metaphysical sciences are taught and practiced with authenticity, integrity, and respect. We strive to build a global ecosystem that promotes trust, ethical standards, and excellence in fields such as Astrology, Numerology, Pranic Healing, Vastu, Tarot, Reiki, and beyond.

Our History

Founded in 2025 by leading practitioners and educators, ICVMS was created to bring standardized accreditation and ethical oversight to these rapidly growing disciplines. As an independent, non-profit body, we have since accredited institutions and practitioners worldwide, building a strong network united by excellence and responsibility

What We Do

ICVMS serves as a global authority in the accreditation and regulation of Vedic and metaphysical sciences.

Accreditation Services

We evaluate and certify institutions, trainers, and practitioners based on rigorous standards of curriculum, teaching quality, and ethical conduct.

Ethical Oversight

Our comprehensive Code of Ethics ensures that accredited members uphold integrity, transparency, and respect in their practice.

Certificate Verification

We provide a transparent and user-friendly verification tool to confirm the authenticity of ICVMS-accredited practitioners and institutions.

Community Building

Through events, webinars, and networking opportunities, we connect practitioners, students, and institutions to foster collaboration and knowledge-sharing.

Global Advocacy

We promote the responsible integration of Vedic and metaphysical sciences into mainstream education and wellness practices, advocating for their recognition and respect.
