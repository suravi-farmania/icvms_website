About the ICVMS

The International Council for Vedic and Metaphysical Studies (ICVMS) is a registered independent accreditation body dedicated to ensuring quality, ethics, and authenticity in the teaching and practice of disciplines such as Astrology, Numerology, Pranic Healing, Vastu, Tarot, Reiki, and other metaphysical sciences.

Our Vision

Preserving Wisdom, Empowering Futures The International Council for Vedic and Metaphysical Sciences (ICVMS) envisions a world where Vedic wisdom and metaphysical sciences are taught and practiced with authenticity, integrity, and respect. We strive.
