About the ICVMS

The International Council for Vedic and Metaphysical Studies (ICVMS) is a registered independent accreditation body dedicated to ensuring quality, ethics, and authenticity in the teaching and practice of disciplines such as Astrology, Numerology, Pranic Healing, Vastu, Tarot, Reiki, and other metaphysical sciences.

Our Mission

Establish ethical and educational standards
Recognize and accredit trusted trainers, practitioners, and institutions
Promote the responsible growth of metaphysical sciences worldwide

By creating a framework of trust, respect, and responsibility, we empower learners, institutions, and communities to benefit from these sciences in a dignified and harmonious way.

The Value of Accreditation with ICVMS

Credibility

Members adhere to a strict Code of Ethics.

Quality Assurance

Accredited institutions meet set benchmarks in teaching and practice.

Recognition

Practitioners gain visibility and trust among students, clients, and the global community.

Community Commitment

Every accredited member contributes to upliftment and harmony.
