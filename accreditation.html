Individual Accreditation

For individual practitioners and scholars, ICVMS certification is a mark of professional credibility and mastery in their respective fields, recognized globally as a standard of excellence.

ICVMS welcomes

  • Individual Practitioners: Certified teachers or practitioners in disciplines like Astrology, Reiki, Vastu, Tarot, Numerology, or other metaphysical fields with at least 2 years of professional experience.

  • Training Institutions: Schools, academies, or organizations offering courses in Vedic or metaphysical sciences, with a structured curriculum and qualified instructors.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Commitment to ICVMS’s Code of Ethics

  • Demonstrated expertise through certifications, testimonials, or documented experience.

  • For institutions: A detailed curriculum, qualified faculty, and evidence of student outcomes

  • Willingness to undergo periodic reviews to maintain accreditation status

Process Overview

1. Submit Application

Complete the online application form, providing details about your qualifications, experience, and (for institutions) curriculum. Include supporting documents like certifications, syllabi, or client testimonials.

2. Review by ICVMS Panel

Our expert accreditors, including field specialists and ethics advisors, evaluate your application against our standards for quality, ethics, and impact.

3. Interview or Audit

Depending on the application type, we may conduct a virtual interview (for individuals) or an audit of teaching practices (for institutions).

4. Decision and Feedback

Receive a decision within 4–6 weeks. Approved applicants are awarded accreditation; others receive constructive feedback for improvement.

5. Ongoing Compliance

Accredited members must renew every 2 years, submitting updates on their practice or curriculum to maintain standards.

Accreditation Tiers

ICVMS offers

  • Individual Practitioner Accreditation

    For solo practitioners or independent teachers. Ideal for those offering consultations or small-scale training

  • Institutional Accreditation

    For schools or academies with structured programs, multiple instructors, or online courses

  • Advanced Accreditation

    For practitioners or institutions with over 5 years of impactful contributions, eligible for leadership roles in ICVMS initiatives. Each tier includes a digital badge, directory listing, and access to ICVMS resources, with varying levels of recognition and responsibilities
